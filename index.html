A little about me!

My name is Alea Abrams and I am a User Experience and Visual Designer.

My passion is design, and I have the solid technical skills to turn my ideas into reality!

I received a BA from Kenyon College, in Gambier OH, graduating with degrees in studio art and psychology. My experience studying psychology allows me to have a deeper understanding of why people find certain things aesthetically pleasing, and combine this understanding with my design and technical skills and experience to create effective products.
